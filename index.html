About Rinco

Rinco International Co., Ltd. is a specialized sustainable bag manufacturer headquartered in Shenzhen, with a factory in Luan City, Anhui Province.

We are committed to providing high-quality, sustainable bag products to global clients using innovative eco-friendly materials such as RPET (recycled polyethylene terephthalate). From design to production, we adhere to the philosophy of "Designed for beauty, crafted with care."

Why Choose RPET?

Today, an increasing number of corporations and retailers opt to use RPET over non-recycled synthetic fiber to reduce the impact on our environment.

🌍

Reduce Plastic Pollution

Each RPET bag recycles 5-10 plastic bottles, reducing landfill and ocean pollution

⚡

Low Carbon Footprint

RPET production reduces energy consumption by ~55% and CO₂ emissions by ~60% vs virgin polyester

🏆

Meet ESG Goals

More brands choose RPET to fulfill corporate social responsibility and enhance brand image

✨

High Quality & Durable

RPET materials undergo strict quality control to ensure durable, beautiful, and practical products
